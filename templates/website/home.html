Welcome to F2 Hotel

A World of Comfort
in the Heart of Kumasi

F2 Hotel & Apartment is a modern, premier hospitality destination located at Akom, Custom Check Point, off the Offinso Road in Ghana's Ashanti Region. We blend contemporary comfort with authentic Ghanaian warmth to deliver an experience our guests never forget.

  • Luxuriously appointed rooms and self-contained apartments
  • Gourmet restaurant and fully stocked bar on-site
  • Sparkling outdoor swimming pool and lounge area
  • Free parking and 24/7 professional front desk service
  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the property
