About F2 Hotel

Luxury & Comfort in Kumasi's Ashanti Region

Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Hospitality

F2 Hotel & Apartment was founded with a singular vision: to create a modern, welcoming sanctuary that gives every guest — whether travelling for business or leisure — a true home away from home in the Ashanti Region of Ghana.

Located at Akom, Custom Check Point, off the Offinso Road in Kumasi, we have become a trusted destination for local and international travellers who seek the perfect balance of luxury, affordability, and warm African hospitality.
